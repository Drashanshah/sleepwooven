Frequently Asked Questions

  • 01
    What are the wash and care instructions?

    To keep our bedding fresh and clean, wash them at least once per week with alternating sets for best results. Use a cold or warm wash cycle and set the dryer to low or medium tumble dry.

    Bleach is not recommended as it destroys the chemical structure of cotton, causing them to be brittle. Use color safe bleach alternatives if necessary and spot-treated required areas.

  • 02
    How should I store my linens

    Always wash bed linens and thoroughly dry them before any long term storage. We naturally shed dead skin cells and leave body oils all over our personal items, especially on our bed sheets if we spend 8 hours a day on them.

    If left uncleaned for long periods of time, they oxidize with cotton which gives off odor and a nasty yellow tint. To prevent mold and mildew from forming, use an airtight vacuum bag to store the linens in a cool and dark place.
