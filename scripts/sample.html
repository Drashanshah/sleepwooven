OneSapon a time, we believe everyone has a purpose and value. We all play a role in society for each other and we all hold the key to drive social change.

Our craftsmen compose of mentally disabled persons and those recovering from mental illnesses. They work at our sheltered workshop with the guidance and care of professional instructors. They build confidence and a sense of accomplishment from collaboration. From ideation, production to packaging, each process is a testimony to their potential and dedication.

Handcrafted soaps like ours have many uses from household chores to bath and body care. Unlike the typical bulk-branded soaps with harsh chemicals that cause damage to our skin and to the world, we pay as much attention to our craftsmen as to our environment. All ingredients are easily understood by you and me and come from all natural sources.

All we are missing is your recognition. Not only are you giving this team an opportunity to work, but you are also making their lives sustainable and independent. Persons with mental conditions have feelings as deep and as rich as any other person. They yearn for the same economic freedom and you are the key to liberate their lives. Feel the passion in every product. For a more equitable society, build it together with us, OneSapon a time.
